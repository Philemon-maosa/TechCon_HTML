History

In 1995, TechCon started out as a modest meetup among software developers in Palo Alto, California.
It was created by a small team of tech enthusiasts eager to exchange ideas and present their projects.
That first event, held at a local community hall, brought together only about 50 participants.

Jamal Okoro

Jamal Okoro is renowned for his groundbreaking work in cybersecurity. As the chief architect behind a
globally adopted secure authentication framework, he has advised governments and corporations alike. At TechCon 2019, he led a panel on the future of digital privacy and encryption.

Past Speakers

Sophia Nguyen

Sophia Nguyen is the founder of GreenTech Innovations and a vocal advocate for sustainable tech. Her work focuses on reducing the carbon footprint of data centers and cloud services. At TechCon 2021, she spoke on eco-conscious engineering and green technology design.

Arjun Patel

Arjun Patel has made significant contributions to the cloud computing ecosystem, particularly through his leadership in several open-source projects. At TechCon 2022, he shared insights on democratizing cloud infrastructure and supporting developer communities worldwide.
